Things to know

Qualifying criteria

To apply for a Premier checking account, you need to have at least one of the following:

  • At least $100,000 in total deposits and/or investments[@investmentsdisclaimershort]
  • At least $5,000 in total direct deposits[@qualdd] per month
  • An HSBC U.S. residential mortgage loan serviced by HSBC
  • Private Bank[@privatebankeligibility] status

You must also:

  • Be at least 18 years old

What you may need to apply 

Some of the things we may ask for include:

  • Your personal, employment, income and tax details
  • Proof of identity such as your passport, valid for a least 6 months
  • Proof of your permanent address, such as a bank statement or utility bill
  • If a student, proof of enrollment
